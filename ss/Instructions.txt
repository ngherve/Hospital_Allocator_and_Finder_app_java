- Please keep the HospitalFile.txt in the same folder as the executable jar file
- When running the program, firstly click on display hospitals button
- Then you can either Add or remove a hospital, or add/remove partnership
- After you can view the details of a hospital by clicking the insight button(to partners and potential partners)
- Select a location and a medical condition to determine the closest hospital to you
- View the map, and refresh for any changes
- Display the hospital
